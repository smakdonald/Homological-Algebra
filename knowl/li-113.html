Item 2.
Let \(\left\{P_{i}\right\}_{i \in I}\) be a family of projective modules. By Theorem 4.9, there exist free modules \(F_{i}\) such that each \(P_{i}\) is a direct summand of \(F_{i}\text{.}\) Therefore, \(\oplus P_{i}\) is a direct summand of \(\oplus_{i} F_{i}\text{,}\) which is also free. By Theorem 4.9, this implies that \(\oplus P_{i}\) is projective.
