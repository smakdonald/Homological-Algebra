Example 2.2. The zero and the identity maps of complexes \(\left(F_{\bullet}, \partial_{\bullet}\right) \longrightarrow\left(F_{\bullet}, \partial_{\bullet}\right)\) are exactly what they sound like: the zero map \(0_{F_{\bullet}}\) is 0 in every homological degree, and the identity map \(1_{F_{\bullet}}\) is the identity in every homological degree.
