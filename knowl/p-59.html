The category \(R\)\(\mod\) of modules over a fixed \(\operatorname{ring} R\) and with \(R\)-module homomorphisms. Sometimes one writes \(R\)\(\Mod\) for this category, and reserve \(R\)\(\mod\) for the category of finitely generated \(R\)-modules with \(R\)\(\Mod\)ule homomorphisms. When \(R=k\) is a field, the objects in the category \(k\)-Mod are \(k\)-vector spaces, and the arrows are linear transformations; we may instead refer to this category as Vect-\(k\text{.}\)
