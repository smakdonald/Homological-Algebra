Exercise 2.4.

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring. Let \(M\) be an \(R\)-module, and \(I\) an ideal in \(R\text{.}\) Then we have the following isomorphisms of \(R\)-modules:
  1. \(\operatorname{Hom}_{R}(R, M) \cong M\text{.}\)
  2. \(\operatorname{Hom}_{R}\left(R^{n}, M\right) \cong M^{n}\) for any \(n \geqslant 1\text{.}\)
  3. \(\operatorname{Hom}_{R}(R / I, M) \cong\left(0:_{M} I\right):=\{m \in M \mid I m=0\}\text{.}\)
