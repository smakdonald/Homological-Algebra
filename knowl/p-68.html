There is a category Set* of pointed sets, where the objects are pairs \((X, x)\) of sets \(X\) and points \(x \in X\text{,}\) and where for two pointed sets \((X, x)\) and \((Y, y)\text{,}\) the morphisms from \((X, x)\) to \((Y, y)\) are functions \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) such that \(f(x)=y\text{,}\) with the usual composition of functions.
