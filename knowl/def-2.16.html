Definition 2.23.

Definition 2.16. A short exact sequence of complexes is an exact complex in \(\mathrm{Ch}(R)\) of the form
\begin{equation*} 0 \longrightarrow A \stackrel{f}{\longrightarrow} B \stackrel{g}{\longrightarrow} C \longrightarrow 0 . \end{equation*}
Equivalently, a short exact sequence of complexes is a commutative diagram
where the rows are exact and the columns are complexes.
