Example 0.11.

Let \(R=k[x] /\left(x^{2}\right)\text{.}\) The following complex is exact:
\begin{equation*} \cdots \longrightarrow R \stackrel{\cdot x}{\longrightarrow} R \stackrel{\cdot x}{\longrightarrow} R \longrightarrow \cdots \end{equation*}
Indeed, the image and the kernel of multiplication by \(x\) are both \((x)\text{.}\)
