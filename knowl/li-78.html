Item 2.a. \(g^{*}\) is injective:.
If \(g^{*}(h)=0\) for some \(h \in \operatorname{Hom}_{R}(C, M)\text{,}\) then \(h g=g^{*}(h)=0\text{.}\) Consider any \(c \in C\text{.}\) Since \(g\) is surjective, there exists \(b \in B\) such that \(g(b)=c\text{.}\) Then \(h(c)=h g(b)=0\text{,}\) so \(h=0\text{.}\)
