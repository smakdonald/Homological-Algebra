Similarly, given any \(r \in R\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} f\left(r a_{1}\right)=r f\left(a_{1}\right)=r h\left(m_{1}\right)=h\left(r m_{1}\right) \end{equation*}
so \(k\left(r m_{1}\right)=r a_{1}=r k\left(m_{1}\right)\text{.}\) Finally, this element \(k \in \operatorname{Hom}_{R}(M, A)\) satisfies
\begin{equation*} f_{*}(k)(m)=f(k(m))=h(m) \end{equation*}
for all \(m \in M\text{,}\) so \(f_{*}(k)=h\) and \(h \in \operatorname{im} f_{*}\text{.}\)
