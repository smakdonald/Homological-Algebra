A right exact contravariant functor \(G\) will take the exact sequence
\begin{equation*} 0 \longrightarrow \operatorname{ker} f \longrightarrow A \stackrel{f}{\longrightarrow} B \end{equation*}
to the exact sequence
\begin{equation*} G(B) \stackrel{G(f)}{\longrightarrow} G(A) \longrightarrow G(\operatorname{ker} f) \longrightarrow 0 \end{equation*}
and exactness says that \(G(\operatorname{ker} f)\) is the cokernel of \(G(f)\text{.}\)
