For all \(R\)-modules \(M, N, M_{i}, N_{i}\text{,}\) there are isomorphisms of abelian groups
\begin{equation*} \operatorname{Hom}_{R}\left(M, \prod_{i} N_{i}\right) \cong \prod_{i} \operatorname{Hom}_{R}\left(M, N_{i}\right) \text { and } \operatorname{Hom}_{R}\left(\bigoplus_{i} M_{i}, N\right) \cong \prod_{i} \operatorname{Hom}_{R}\left(M_{i}, N\right) \end{equation*}
Moreover, when \(R\) is commutative, these are in fact isomorphisms of \(R\)-modules.
