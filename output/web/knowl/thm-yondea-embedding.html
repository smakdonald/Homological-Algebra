Theorem 1.45. Yoneda Embedding.

Let \(\mathscr{C}\) be a locally small category. The covariant functor
from \(\mathscr{C}\) to the category of contravariant functors \(\mathscr{C} \longrightarrow\) Set is an embedding. Moreover, the contravariant functor
from the category \(\mathscr{C}\) to the category of covariant functors \(\mathscr{C} \longrightarrow\) Set is also an embedding.

Proof.
