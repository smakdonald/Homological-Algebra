Given a family of \(R\)-module homomorphisms \(f_{i}: M_{i} \longrightarrow N_{i}\text{,}\) there is an \(R\)-module homomorphism
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{r} \bigoplus_{i \in I} M_{i} \stackrel{\left(f_{i}\right)_{i \in I}}{\longmapsto} \bigoplus_{i \in I} N_{i} \\ \quad\left(m_{i}\right) \longmapsto \end{array} \end{equation*}
which is injective if and only if every \(f_{i}\) is injective.
