Remark A.10. If \(R\) is a commutative ring, then any left \(R\)-module \(M\) may be regarded as a right \(R\)-module by setting \(m r:=r m\text{.}\) Likewise, any right \(R\)-module may be regarded as a left \(R\)-module. Thus for commutative rings, we just refer to modules, and not left or right modules.
