Suppose \(M \oplus A \cong P\) for some projective module \(P\text{.}\) By Theorem 4.9, there exists a free \(R\)-module \(F\) and an \(R\)-module \(B\) such that \(P \oplus B \cong F\text{.}\) Then \(M \oplus A \oplus B \cong P \oplus B \cong F\text{,}\) and by Theorem 4.9 this implies \(M\) is projective.
