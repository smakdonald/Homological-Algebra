Now we take \(N\) to be the finitely generated submodule of \(M\) generated by \(m_{1}, \ldots, m_{n}\) and \(c_{1}, \ldots, c_{t}\text{,}\) and \(v=m_{1} \otimes a_{1}+\cdots+m_{n} \otimes a_{n} \in N \otimes A\text{.}\) Now
\begin{equation*} \left(j \otimes 1_{A}\right)(v)=\left(j \otimes 1_{A}\right)\left(m_{1} \otimes a_{1}+\cdots+m_{n} \otimes a_{n}\right)=m_{1} \otimes a_{1}+\cdots+m_{n} \otimes a_{n} \in M \otimes_{R} A, \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} \left(1_{N} \otimes i\right)(v)=\left(1_{N} \otimes i\right)\left(m_{1} \otimes a_{1}+\cdots+m_{n} \otimes a_{n}\right)=m_{1} \otimes i\left(a_{1}\right)+\cdots+m_{n} \otimes i\left(a_{n}\right)=0 \end{equation*}
as desired.
