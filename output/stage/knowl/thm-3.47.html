Theorem 3.63.

Theorem 3.47. Let \(M\) be a right \(R\)-module. The functor \(M \otimes_{R}-\) is right exact, meaning that for every exact sequence
\begin{equation*} A \stackrel{i}{\longrightarrow} B \stackrel{p}{\longrightarrow} C \longrightarrow 0 \end{equation*}
the sequence
\begin{equation*} M \otimes_{R} A \stackrel{1 \otimes i}{\longrightarrow} M \otimes_{R} B \stackrel{1 \otimes p}{\longrightarrow} M \otimes_{R} C \longrightarrow 0 \end{equation*}
is exact.

Proof.
