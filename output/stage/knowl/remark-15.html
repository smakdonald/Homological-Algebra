Remark 2.28.

Remark 2.21. In the split short exact sequence
\begin{equation*} 0 \longrightarrow A \stackrel{i}{\longrightarrow} A \oplus C \stackrel{p}{\longrightarrow} C \longrightarrow 0 \end{equation*}
the canonical projection \(q: A \oplus C \rightarrow A\) and the usual inclusion \(r: C \rightarrow A \oplus C\) are splittings.
