Item 1.
The covariant functor \(\operatorname{Hom}_{R}(M,-)\) is left exact: for every exact sequence
\begin{equation*} 0 \longrightarrow A \stackrel{f}{\longrightarrow} B \stackrel{g}{\longrightarrow} C \end{equation*}
of \(R\)-modules, the sequence
\begin{equation*} 0 \longrightarrow \operatorname{Hom}_{R}(M, A) \stackrel{\operatorname{Hom}_{R}(M, f)}{\longrightarrow} \operatorname{Hom}_{R}(M, B) \stackrel{\operatorname{Hom}_{R}(M, g)}{\longrightarrow} \operatorname{Hom}_{R}(M, C) \end{equation*}
is exact.
