Theorem 1.3.6.

Theorem 1.41 (Yoneda Lemma). Let \(\mathscr{C}\) be a locally small category, and fix an object \(A\) in \(\mathscr{C}\text{.}\) Let \(F: \mathscr{C} \longrightarrow\) Set be a covariant functor. Then there is a bijection
\begin{equation*} \operatorname{Nat}\left(\operatorname{Hom}_{\mathscr{C}}(A,-), F\right) \stackrel{\gamma}{\longrightarrow} F(A) \end{equation*}
Moreover, this correspondence is natural in both \(A\) and \(F\text{.}\)

Proof.
